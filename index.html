🛼 10 Days of Roller Skating Summer Camp

Join us for an exciting 10-day journey to master the art of rolling! This camp is designed specifically for absolute beginners of all ages at the beautiful Chaliyam Beach, Kozhikode.

📍 Location & Details

Venue: Chaliyam Beach, Kozhikode

Age Group: 4 years to No Limit

Registration Fee: ₹1499

Date: April 1 to 10

⏰ Schedule

Batch 1: 7:00 AM – 8:30 AM

Batch 2: 8:45 AM – 10:15 AM

🛡️ Required Equipment

  • Skating Shoes
  • Helmet
  • Knee Pads
  • Elbow Pads
  • Wrist Guards

📘 10-Day Beginner Curriculum

  • Day 1: Gear Safety & Learning to Fall / Stand
  • Day 2: Balance & Posture
  • Day 3: Duck Walk
  • Day 4: Gliding & Weight Shifting
  • Day 5: Plow Stop
  • Day 6: Turning Techniques
  • Day 7: Continuous Motion
  • Day 8: Agility Drills
  • Day 9: T Stop
  • Day 10: Graduation
